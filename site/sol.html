About

Sol Unified is a personal project — a labor of love built on nights and weekends. I made it because I was tired of context switching between apps and constantly copy-pasting into AI tools. Everything stays local on your machine. No cloud, no subscription, no tracking.

It's open-source and free. If you find it useful, consider contributing on GitHub. If you want to reach out, you can find me at savarsareen.com.
